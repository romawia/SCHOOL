CHAPTER 1 : RESOURCES AND THEIR DEVELOPMENT

A HNUAIA MI HI ULUK TAKIN CHHIAR LA, I HRIATTHIAM HMA CHU CHHIAR NAWN LEH THIN ANG CHE

Mihring te thiamna leh hmsawnna avanga leilunga thil awmsa kan tana tangkai tura kan buatsaih hian chu'ng (chutiang) leilunga thil awmsa te chu RESOURCES tih an ni a. Mihringte tihdanglam ngai kher lova tangkaina nei sa(Thing, Ni eng, Boruak etc) te pawh RESOURCE an ni vek tho tihna ani. Tangkaina neilo leh mamawhna min phuhruk theilo chu RESOURCES an nilo tihna a nih chu. Entir nan, Chota Nagpur Plateau vela mineral tam tak inphum te pawh khu Iron and Steel Industry a din hma kha chuan engtihna mah an ni lova. Iron and Steel Industry a din hnuah chuan chu’ng mineral te chu RESOURCE tih an lo ni ta a ni. A tawi zawngin TANGKAINA nei apiang hi RESOURCE kan tih chu a ni.
Nawhalh far khat te mai khan Sir Krosa chuan meizial a zuk theih phah a, chuvangin nawhalh far khat chu Sir Krosa tan chuan RESOURCE a ni. Kei erawh chuan meizial ka zuk ngai loh avangin nawhalh far khat chu RESOURCE a tling ve lo tihna a nih chu. Mamawh tu a zirin thil reng reng RESOURCE an tling in an tling lo thei bawk tihna a nih tak chu. Mihringte changkanna a zirin RESOURCE kan tih pawh chu a danglam ve thei bawk a. Thingtlang lo nei mi tan chuan CHEMPUI hi resource a ni. Computer engineer tan erawh chuan CHEMPUI chu resource a ni miah lo a. Tichuan RESOURCE nihphung leh a nihdan te kan hrethiam tawh ang chu. Thil pakhat chiang ta em em chu RESOURCE kan tih te reng reng hian mihring te nunphung a ti awlsam in a ti hahdam thin.
RESOURCE THENDARH DAN CHI HRANG HRANG TE :

1. NATURAL RESOURCES
Leilunga thil awmsa emaw Pathian thilsiam amah a tangkaina nei leh tihdanglam ngai lova kan hman theih te reng reng hi NATURAL RESOURCE kan tih te chu a ni. E.g, Land, Water, Soil,Mineral, Forest etc.
2. HUMAN-MADE RESOURCES / CULTURAL RESOURCES
Mihringten kan tana tangkaina nei tura kan thilsiam te reng reng hi Human-made(mihring siam) Resource kan tih te chu an ni. Mihringte changkan-na avangin chu’ng(chutiang) kan thilsiam te pawh chu a danglam in a inang lo thei viau bawk ang. E.g Building, Bridge,Transport-line etc
